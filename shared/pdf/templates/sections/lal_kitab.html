Lal Kitab — House by House

The North-Indian Practical Remedy System

Lal Kitab ("Red Book") is a 19th-century North-Indian astrology + remedy tradition. Its practices are simple, household-level actions — feed crows, keep silver, respect mother, plant a tree — designed to neutralise heavy karma without elaborate ritual.
