Shodashavarga — 16 Divisional Charts

Each lens reveals one life domain

The Shodashavarga are sixteen sub-divisions of the birth chart, each focused on one specific life area. Read alongside D1 (Lagna chart), they refine what the birth chart promises. D9 (Navamsha — marriage) and D60 (Shashtiamsha — past actions) are the most decisive.
